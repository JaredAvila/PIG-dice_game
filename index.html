Game Rules

  1. The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  2. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they wish. Each result will get added to their ROUND score
  3. If the player rolls a 1, all their ROUND score is lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  4. At any point the player can choose to 'Hold', which means that their ROUND score gets added to their GLBAL score, and it would then be the other players turn.
  5. If at any point a player rolls two 6's in a row they lose their entire score and start over at 0 again.
  6. The first player to reach 100 points on their GLOBAL score wins the game
  7. GLHF! (Good luck, have fun)
